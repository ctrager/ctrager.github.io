Corey Trager's HTML/Javascript Music Tools

Some music apps implemented as single html files.
They all work offline.
Mostly tested with desktop Chrome, but they probably work in other browsers and on mobile. Let me know what you think at ctrager@yahoo.com

Metronome.html - a pretty decent metronome
Drone Tones - plays drone tones, which can help with violin intonation
Pitch Detector - (work in progress) tries to tell you whether you are playing in tune
